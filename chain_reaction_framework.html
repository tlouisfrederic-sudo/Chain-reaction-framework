Gold Macro Trading · June 2026
Chain Reaction Framework
How any macro event flows through to gold — read this after any data release or headline, in order, top to bottom
EVENT → Surprise vs consensus → Bond curve shape → Decompose (nominals vs breakevens → real yields) → DXY confirms or contradicts → Oil chain active or reversing → Fed hike probability up or down → Fear premium adds or removes → Channels aligned = TRADE · Channels fighting = WAIT
0
Expectation Theory — Before anything else
ALWAYS FIRST
The only question
Did this beat, miss, or match consensus — and by how much?
Why it matters
Markets price the expected future state continuously. The move is always relative to consensus, never absolute. A hot print that was fully expected = no move. A surprise = large move.
Ask yourself
Is this a one-off or does it confirm a trend? Was this already partially priced from earlier data this week?
1
Bonds — React first, read the curve
BONDS MOVE FIRST
2yr yield
The Fed signal. Rises when market prices a hike. Falls when market prices a cut. Your real-time hike probability indicator.
10yr yield
Growth and inflation signal. Rises on hot inflation or strong growth. Falls on growth fear or flight to safety.
30yr yield
Purest long-term inflation signal. Less sensitive to Fed decisions than the front end.
Curve shape
Bear flattening (front end up more) = hiking regime = bearish gold. Bull steepening (both down, front end more) = Fed cutting = most bullish gold.

3
DXY — The amplifier
MECHANICAL HEADWIND / TAILWIND
The mechanism
Gold is priced in dollars. Dollar up = gold more expensive globally = foreign demand falls = price falls. Dollar down = opposite.
Rate differential
Higher US rates vs rest of world → capital flows to dollar → DXY strengthens → headwind for gold.
Key level
DXY 200-day EMA. Above it = dollar regime intact = ceiling active. Below it = dollar breaking down = ceiling removed. Gold cannot rally while DXY is above 200-day in a rising real yield environment.
Alignment check
Bonds hawkish + DXY up = double bearish gold. Bonds dovish + DXY down = double bullish. Divergence = wait.

4
Oil — Inflation chain transmission
4-6 WEEK LAG
The chain
Oil up → CPI up in 4-6 weeks → Fed stays hawkish → real yields stay elevated → gold capped.
Reverse chain
Oil down → CPI down in 4-6 weeks → Fed can cut eventually → real yields compress → gold ceiling lifts.
Key levels
WTI above $90 = inflation chain active. Below $85 sustained = disinflation pathway opening. Each $10 fall removes ~0.25% CPI.
The paradox
Oil up on Iran escalation = bearish gold via inflation chain AND bullish via fear premium. Channels fight. Gold goes sideways. This is why gold was range-bound with an active war.
2026 paradox
Strikes cancelled = gold UP. Inflation chain tail risk removal (avoiding oil $140, CPI 7%) is worth more than the war premium loss. Net bullish in stagflation regime.
5
Fed Channel — Bridge between data and gold
HIKE / CUT / HOLD
Three states
Easing (cutting) = real yields fall = ceiling lifts = most bullish. Frozen (on hold) = real yields stable = range. Tightening (hiking) = real yields rise = ceiling tightens = most bearish.
Read it daily
Check December hike probability before every session. If it moved overnight — why and what changed.

Fear Channel — War premium and geopolitical
OPERATES INDEPENDENTLY
Three layers
Structural de-dollarisation ($1,000-1,500) — does not move on headlines. Iran war premium ($200-300) — moves on deal/escalation. Active risk spike — fast in, fast out, 24-48h.
Exit condition
War premium exits ONLY when tankers physically move through a reopened Strait. Not on a draft MoU. Not on headlines. Physical evidence only.
Fear dominates when
Sudden escalation or unexpected ceasefire collapse. Fear channel overwhelms macro channels temporarily. Active spike: do not chase.

7
Final Read — Are the channels aligned or fighting?
TRADE DECISION
All channels bearish
Hot data + real yields up + DXY up + oil up + hike priced. Sell gold with conviction.
All channels bullish
Soft data + real yields down + DXY down + oil down + cuts return. Buy gold with conviction.
Channels mixed / fighting
Fear channel bullish but Fed channel bearish — or vice versa. Range holds. Wait. No trade.
01
Did real yields move and which direction?
Ceiling tighter or looser — this is the primary gold signal. DFII10 direction is the tiebreaker when channels are mixed.
02
Is the fear channel adding or removing premium?
Floor higher or lower — war premium still intact, partially exited, or fully priced out. Physical evidence only for exit.
03
Are the channels aligned or fighting?
Aligned = trade with conviction, size up. Fighting = wait for resolution. One dominant, others neutral = moderate conviction, smaller size.
Real yields are the tiebreaker. When unsure which channel is dominant — check DFII10. Whichever direction it moved is the answer. Everything else is context around the primary ceiling signal.
